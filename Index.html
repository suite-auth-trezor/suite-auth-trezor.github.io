What is Trezor.io/Start?


To set up your Trezor device, visit trezor.io/start and follow the simple instructions. Begin by connecting your device to your computer using the USB cable. You'll be prompted to install the Trezor Bridge, which allows communication between the device and your browser. Once installed, follow the on-screen instructions to initialize your Trezor. You'll create a new wallet or recover an existing one using your recovery seed. Ensure you securely store your seed phrase, as it's essential for wallet recovery. After setup, you can access and manage your crypto assets through Trezor Suite.

Setting up Trezor Bridge

  1. Visit trezor.io/start: Connect your Trezor device to your computer via USB and visit trezor.io/start.
  2. Download Trezor Bridge: You'll be prompted to download Trezor Bridge, the software that enables your Trezor device to communicate with your computer.
  3. Install the Software: Follow the installation instructions specific to your operating system (Windows, macOS, or Linux).
  4. Verify Installation: After installation, Trezor Bridge should automatically start running in the background. You can verify it's running by checking for the Trezor icon in your system tray or menu bar.
  5. Connect to Trezor Suite: With Trezor Bridge running, return to Trezor Suite and refresh the page. Your device should now be recognized, allowing you to continue the setup process.

How to Install Trezor Bridge

  1. Download Trezor Bridge: Visit the Trezor website and navigate to the software section. Download Trezor Bridge for your operating system (Windows, macOS, or Linux).
  2. Install Trezor Bridge: Open the downloaded file and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation process.

Setting Up Trezor Bridge

  • Connect Your Trezor Device: Plug your Trezor device into a USB port on your computer using the provided cable.
  • Open Trezor Wallet: Launch the Trezor Wallet application in your browser. It should automatically detect that Trezor Bridge is installed.
  • Verify Connection: Your Trezor device should now be recognized by the Trezor Wallet. Follow any additional prompts to complete the setup.

Troubleshooting Trezor Bridge

  • Verify that Trezor Bridge is installed correctly. If you encounter issues, reinstall the software.
  • Make sure you have the latest version of Trezor Bridge. Check for updates on the Trezor website.
  • Ensure that the USB cable and port are functioning correctly. Try connecting to a different port or using a different cable if necessary.
  • Trezor Bridge is compatible with most modern browsers. Ensure you’re using a supported browser and try clearing your browser cache if problems persist.

By following these steps, you should be able to effectively use Trezor Bridge to manage your Trezor hardware wallet.
